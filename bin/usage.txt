.
  ---------------------
  Azk benchmarking tool
  ---------------------

  - You need azk instaled. Folow this link to learn how: http://www.azk.io/

  - You may configure this tool telling where does azk binnary is.
  Copy `.env_example` to `.env` and edit `.env` file:

  $ cp .env_example .env
  $ vim .env
.

Usage:
  azk-benchmark [--send]
  azk-benchmark -h | --help
  azk-benchmark --version

Options:
  -h --help     Show this help.
  --version     Show version.
  --send        Send results to Keen.IO
